## 2. Overview

DeskFit is a desk fitness reminder app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

**Key principle:** The App stores almost all data locally on your device. We do not operate our own servers. We do not collect or store personal health data on any external server.

---

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device (SharedPreferences)

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

- **Exercise & workout data:** Break counts, workout durations, exercise categories, streaks
- **Mood journal entries:** Daily mood ratings (1–5 scale) with dates
- **App settings:** Notification schedule, working hours, exercise preferences, pause duration
- **Onboarding answers:** Occupation type, sitting hours, pain areas, activity level, equipment preferences
- **Custom workouts:** User-created exercise routines

This data is local only. It is lost if you uninstall the App or reset your device. We have no access to it.

### 3.2 Data Collected by RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

To process subscriptions and one-time purchases, the App uses **RevenueCat, Inc.** (548 Market St PMB 90303, San Francisco, CA 94104-5401, USA).

RevenueCat may collect and process:

- A non-personal App User ID (randomly generated, not linked to your identity by us)
- Purchase transaction data (subscription type, purchase date, renewal status, trial status)
- Device identifiers (e.g., IDFV on iOS, Android ID on Android)
- Store receipt data (from Apple App Store or Google Play Store)

RevenueCat acts as our data processor. Your payment information (credit card, etc.) is processed exclusively by Apple or Google — we never see it.

For details on RevenueCat's data practices, see: [https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

### 3.3 Local Notifications

The App uses **flutter_local_notifications** to send break reminders to your device. These are fully local — no notification server is involved, and no data leaves your device for this purpose.

### 3.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Name, email address, or any account information (there is no account)
- Location data
- Precise health or biometric data
- Browsing history or device usage data
- Any data through analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)

## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU/EEA resident, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data.
- **Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"):** Request deletion of your data.
- **Right to restriction:** Request that we restrict processing of your data.
- **Right to data portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Right to object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Withdraw notification permission at any time in your device settings.

To exercise these rights, contact: valentinmuenchow@gmail.com

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. In Germany: **Bundesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit (BfDI)**, [https://www.bfdi.bund.de](https://www.bfdi.bund.de).
